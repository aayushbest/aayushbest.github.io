An accidental experiment that touched millions of heart:Butter Chicken

The history of Butter Chicken aka Murgh Makhani in Hindi, starts post partition of India in 1947. A guy named Kundan Lal Gujral who was working in a dhaba called Mokhe Da Dhaba in Lahore before Partition moved to Dehli post partition and setup its outlet called Moti Mahal where he used to sell Tandoori Chicken(that's a story of some other day) but one of the challanges he faced that tandoori chicken which are not sold till evening gets dried and is of no use. He found a way he made a tomato and cream base gravy with assorted aromatic indian spicies and tossed the dried tandoori chicken in that and that's how Butter Chicken was born.
